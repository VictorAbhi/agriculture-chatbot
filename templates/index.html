What causes damping-off in seedlings? 19:23
Damping-off is caused by pre-emergence damping off and seed rotting, and post-emergence damping off. Pre-emergence damping off and seed rotting cause poor seedling stands. Post-emergence damping off infects seedlings near the soil line after emergence, causing them to become soft, water-soaked, and weak, leading to their death.
